Hello I'm Mitch Flood

Hello, my name is Mitch Flood and I am a 27 year old residing in Denver, CO. I started my professional life as an electrician where I was consistently trouble shooting to find solutions to the problem at hand. I loved the technical creativity of being an electrician but decided it was time to look for a less life threatening career. As I looked for a career change, I knew I wanted to continue to utilize my problem solving skills in my next role. This led me to coding. I've recently finished the full stack web development boot camp at DU. I love the never ending challenge that is coding and look forward to developing my skills even further. My most recent accomplishment is developing a rock climbing partner finding application, called Belay Buddy. I utilized React, Tailwind CSS, Mongodb and Node. Specifically, I was responsible for creating the initial application concept wire frames, the rock climbing gym and user database along with front end styling. Innovative problem-solver who is passionate about developing apps with a focus on mobile-first design and development. Strengths in creativity, teamwork, and building projects from ideation to execution. After coding boot camp I wanted to continue to develop my skills while on the job hunt so I decided to complete free code camp as well.
